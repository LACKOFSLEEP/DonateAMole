ABOUT THE APP

Here at Donate A Mole, we are driven by a single goal; to do our part in making the world a better place for all. Since there are currently insufficient quality images and data available for researchers to analyse, the aim of this product is to collect as many mole images and detailed mole information as possible from the general public. The purpose of collecting mole images is for Dermatology Research Centre to create a crowd sourced database of moles to analyse mole images and conduct further research on melanomas to reach our final goal: a world without Melanoma.
